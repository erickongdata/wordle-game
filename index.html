Wordle

How to play

Guess the word in 6 attempts.

GREEN tiles are correct letters.

YELLOW tiles are letters in the wrong place.

DARK tiles are letters that aren't present.

Difficulty levels

You can change the difficulty at the start of each game. Higher levels add on more rules.

Easy

Each word must be valid.

Medium

All revealed letters must be used in the next attempt.

Hard

All revealed correct letters must be used in their shown places.
