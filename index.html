Wordle

How to play

Guess the word in six attempts.

At the start, type any valid five-letter word and press Enter.

Revealed letters are colored based on how good the guess is.

S P E A K

The letter S is in the right place.

Letters P and A are in the wrong place.

Letters E and K are not in the word.

Hard mode

Any revealed letters must be used on the next try.
