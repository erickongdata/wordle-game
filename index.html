Wordle

How to play

Guess the word in six attempts.

At the start, type any valid five-letter word and press Enter.

Revealed letters are colored based on how good the guess is.

S P E A K

The letter S is in the right place.

Letters P and A are in the wrong place.

Letters E and K are not in the word.

Difficulty levels

You can change the difficulty at the start of each game. Higher levels add on more rules.

Easy

Each word must be valid.

Medium

Any revealed letters must be used in the next attempt.

Hard

Any revealed correct letters must be used in their shown places.
